Our History

New Life Deliverance Church – Get Delivered and Enter Into a New Life

New Life Deliverance Church stems from Deliverance International Outreach Ministries, established over a decade ago. After years of dedicated ministry, New Life Deliverance Church was birthed and has now been serving God's people for about 3 to 4 years.

Located in Nakinyuguzi zone, Makindye Luwafu along Salama Road in Kampala, Uganda, the church is spiritually led by Apostle John Bunjo and shepherded by Senior Pastor Joseph Kabuye with assistance from Pastor Judith Babirye.

With over 400 members, our ministry includes church planting like New Life Deliverance Church Gangu, led by Pastor Lugolobi Paul and his wife Nabuuza Margret. Our mission emphasizes deliverance, evangelism, and charitable outreach including door-to-door ministry, care for widows, orphans, and the elderly.

We’ve conducted crusades and conferences both locally and internationally—Jinja, Goma (DRC), and Kasangati—and we run a weekly radio program every Saturday on Family FM 103.5.
